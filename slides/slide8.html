A Warning for Researchers

Critical Insight
The Metric Trap

Traditional no-reference metrics like NIQE, BRISQUE, and PIQE don't fully reflect human perception. These metrics optimize for numerical scores rather than visual quality.

NIQE
BRISQUE
PIQE

Key Issue: Images that score high on these metrics often look unnatural to human observers, with artifacts like over–smoothing and halo effects.
The Perception Gap

Current evaluation methods focus on statistical measures rather than human visual experience. We need perception-aware systems that prioritize natural–looking results.

Call to Action

Build and evaluate with perception–aware systems and user studies. Move beyond score–chasing to create truly better visual experiences.
